About Me

I’m a software engineer who designs systems from the ground up — from high-level architecture to low-level implementation. With deep expertise in HLD/LLD, strong multi-language coding skills, and a passion for data structures and algorithms, I focus on building scalable, real-time solutions for challenging problems.

"Design first. Code with purpose. Ship with confidence."

🏗️ System Design Philosophy

I believe great systems are born from clarity, scalability, and resilience, not unnecessary complexity. My approach balances technical depth with business practicality, ensuring solutions are both future-ready and cost-effective.

  • 📐 High-Level Design (HLD): Defining service boundaries, API flows, fault tolerance, and scalability strategies.
  • 🔧 Low-Level Design (LLD): Crafting class models, data structures, threading, and memory-efficient logic.
  • ⚙️ Real-time Architectures: Designing EV charging & IoT platforms with concurrency, messaging queues, and reliable protocols.
  • ☁️ Cloud-Native Systems: Leveraging AWS services while balancing cost and performance.
  • 🧩 Design Patterns: Applying patterns like Strategy, Observer, and Facade to simplify complexity and increase maintainability.

Whether it's an MVP or a globally distributed platform, I design with a focus on scalability, maintainability, and real-world constraints.
