About Me

I’m a Software Engineer passionate about building technology that powers real-world infrastructure. From backend platforms to cloud-native services, I specialize in designing, coding, and scaling systems that are reliable, efficient, and business-driven. My journey has taken me from writing core frameworks to architecting EV charging solutions used across India.

"Design first. Code with purpose. Ship with confidence."

🏗️ System Design Philosophy

I believe great systems are born from clarity, scalability, and resilience, not unnecessary complexity. My approach balances technical depth with business practicality, ensuring solutions are both future-ready and cost-effective.

  • 📐 High-Level Design (HLD): Service boundaries, API flows, fault tolerance & scalability strategies
  • 🔧 Low-Level Design (LLD): Class modeling, data structures, memory efficiency & threading
  • ⚙️ Real-time Architectures: EV charging & IoT platforms with concurrency, messaging queues & protocols
  • ☁️ Cloud-Native Systems: Using AWS to design cost-aware, high-performance deployments
  • 🧩 Design Patterns: Strategy, Observer, Adapter & Facade applied to simplify and scale systems

Whether it’s an MVP or a globally distributed system, my design process always focuses on scalability, maintainability, and real-world constraints.
