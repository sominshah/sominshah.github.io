EV Charging Management System (CMS)

A full-stack, production-grade EV charging platform built from scratch — managing partners, charging stations, real-time OCPP communication, billing, tariffs, and end customer wallets. Deployed and live at evtechs.in.

  • ✔️ Real-time charger monitoring with auto-refresh live status board
  • ✔️ Remote OCPP 1.6 operations — Start/Stop, Reset, Trigger, Unlock, Get Config
  • ✔️ Full OCPP message log viewer with filter by charger, action & date range
  • ✔️ Multi-partner architecture with stations, connectors, and credential management
  • ✔️ B2C tariff engine with global and custom tariff scopes
  • ✔️ End customer wallet — credits, debits, session history & invoices
  • ✔️ OCPI Network Board for EV roaming setup (Platform → Role → Connection → Modules)
  • ✔️ Business dashboard with session/revenue trend charts and operational alerts
  • ✔️ EV & Charger OEM catalogue with vehicle and manufacturer management
